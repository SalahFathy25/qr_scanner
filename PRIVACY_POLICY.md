## English

### Introduction

Salah Fathy ("**we**", "**us**", or "**our**") operates the **QR Studio** mobile application (the "**App**").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with its terms, please do not use the App.

We reserve the right to update this policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

---

### 1. Information We Collect

#### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **QR Code Content**: Data you enter to generate QR codes (text, URLs, WiFi credentials, contact info, calendar events, etc.).
- **Scan History**: A log of QR codes you scan via the camera or gallery.

#### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Camera Feed**: Accessed in real time for QR scanning. No images, videos, or frames are captured, stored, or transmitted off your device.
- **App Preferences**: Theme choice (System/Light/Dark), default QR color, auto-open scanner setting, vibrate on scan setting — stored locally.

#### 1.3 No Account or Personal Information
We do **not** require an account, registration, email, phone number, or any personally identifiable information (PII) to use the App.

---

### 2. How We Use Your Information

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| QR code content (generated) | Display, share, save, and export your QR codes |
| Scan history | Allow you to review previously scanned codes |
| Camera feed | Real-time QR code detection only |
| Preferences | Personalize your app experience |
| App version | Display in "About" section |

---

### 3. Data Storage & Security

- **All data is stored locally on your device** using `SharedPreferences` (Android).
- QR code images you save are stored in your device's local documents directory.
- **No data is transmitted** to any external server, cloud, or third party.
- You can delete all your data at any time via the **Clear All QR Codes** and **Clear Scan History** options in Settings.

---

### 4. Permissions

| Permission | Purpose | Persistent? |
|------------|---------|-------------|
| **Camera** | Scan QR codes in real time | Only while scanning |
| **Internet** | Load Google Fonts (Poppins) and open URLs you tap | As needed |

We do **not** request or access:
- Contacts
- Location (GPS)
- SMS / Call logs
- Microphone
- Photos / Media (except when you explicitly pick a gallery image for QR decoding)

---

### 5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party packages, each with its own data handling:

| Package | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| `google_fonts` | Load Poppins font from Google CDN | Device IP (standard web request); **no user data** |
| `url_launcher` | Open URLs / emails in external apps | None; launches system browser or email client |
| `share_plus` | Share QR codes via OS share sheet | None; uses native sharing UI |
| `qr_flutter` | Render QR codes | None; runs entirely on-device |
| `qr_code_scanner_plus` | Camera QR scanning | None; processes camera frames on-device only |
| `zxing2` | Decode QR from gallery images | None; runs entirely on-device |

We do **not** use any analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or social media tracking SDKs.

---

### 6. Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with data, please contact us so we can delete it.

---

### 7. Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time:
- **From within the App**: Settings → Data → Clear All QR Codes / Clear Scan History
- **From your device**: Android Settings → Apps → QR Studio → Storage → Clear Data
- **Uninstalling** the App will also remove all locally stored data.

### مقدمة

يقوم تطبيق **QR Studio** (المشار إليه بـ"**التطبيق**") بتشغيله المطور **صلاح فتحي** ("**نحن**" أو "**خاصتنا**").

توضح سياسة الخصوصية هذه كيفية جمعنا واستخدامنا وحمايتنا لمعلوماتك عند استخدام تطبيقنا. يرجى قراءة هذه السياسة بعناية.

### ١. المعلومات التي نجمعها

#### ١.١ المعلومات التي تقدمها مباشرة
- **محتوى رمز QR**: البيانات التي تدخلها لإنشاء رموز QR (نصوص، روابط، بيانات WiFi، معلومات الاتصال، أحداث التقويم، إلخ).
- **سجل المسح**: سجل برموز QR التي قمت بمسحها ضوئياً عبر الكاميرا أو معرض الصور.

#### ١.٢ المعلومات التي يتم جمعها تلقائياً
- **كاميرا الجهاز**: يتم الوصول إليها في الوقت الفعلي لمسح رموز QR فقط. لا يتم التقاط أو تخزين أو إرسال أي صور أو فيديوهات من جهازك.
- **إعدادات التطبيق**: اختيار السمة (نظامي / فاتح / داكن)، لون QR الافتراضي، إعدادات المسح التلقائي — جميعها مخزنة محلياً.

#### ١.٣ لا توجد معلومات حساب أو معلومات شخصية
**لا** يتطلب التطبيق إنشاء حساب أو تسجيل أو بريد إلكتروني أو رقم هاتف أو أي معلومات تعريف شخصية لاستخدامه.
